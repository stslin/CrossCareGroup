# Privacy Commitment

Handling your clinical data with the highest standards of enterprise integrity and transparency.

### 1\. Commitment to Privacy

We respect your privacy and handle personal information with the highest level of clinical integrity. This document outlines our data standards for the Cross Care clinical ecosystem.

#### 

Data Collection

We collect information provided during intake and clinical transition tasks, including clinical records and assessment notes required for safe discharge.

#### 

Data Security

Cross Care Group is a Microsoft-first organization. All clinical data is stored in healthcare-standard encrypted environments with multi-factor audit paths.

### 2\. Clinical Disclosure

Information may be shared only with defined care team members (Doctors, OT, Registered Nurses) as part of your integrated care plan. We never disclose data for marketing purposes.

### 3\. Your Rights

You have the right to request a copy of your clinical data held by the Cross Care Group at any time. Requests are generally processed within 2 business days.
