About Us

At Cross Care Group, we offer personalised, client-centric healthcare, tailored to each individual’s needs. Our family-centered approach involves loved ones in creating comprehensive care plans, developed in collaboration with our multidisciplinary team of medical professionals. This ensures holistic well-being and community integration for every client. We acknowledge that personalised healthcare is essential, as no single approach fits all. Emphasising the importance of community for overall well-being, our multidisciplinary team of healthcare professionals collaborates to develop tailored care plans through individual and group consultations to meet your specific needs.

Our Approach

At Cross Care Group, we take a person-centered approach to care, placing your needs & well-being at the heart of everything we do. Our philosophy is built on the following pillars:

Holistic Care

We believe in providing holistic care that addresses all aspects of your well-being. Our team of dedicated professionals, including highly skilled Nurses, Care and Allied Health professionals, work collaboratively to design and deliver tailored support services that encompass physical, emotional, and social needs.

Individualized Support Plans

We recognize that every individual is unique, with different strengths, challenges, and aspirations. Our experienced team takes the time to understand your specific requirements, preferences, and goals, enabling us to create personalized support plans that cater to your individual needs. ​

Qualified and Compassionate Team

We pride ourselves on having a highly skilled team of qualified professionals who are passionate about making a difference in the lives of participants with high and complex needs. Our staff undergo rigorous training and are equipped with the knowledge, expertise, and empathy necessary to deliver compassionate care.

Continuity and Collaboration

We value the importance of continuity in care provision. Through effective communication and collaboration, we ensure a seamless transition between our care professionals, facilitating consistent and coordinated support. This approach fosters trust, enhances the quality of care, and promotes a positive care experience.
