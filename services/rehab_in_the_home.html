Rehab Clarifications

Rehabilitation FAQs

Is Rehab-in-the-Home available everywhere?

Availability varies by region and clinical cohort as our services expand. We confirm availability during our triage and referral review process.

How is this different to Allied Health?

Rehab-in-the-Home is a structured, time-limited acute recovery pathway with specific functional milestones. General Allied Health services may support longer-term functional independence and maintenance once rehab goals are met.

Will you communicate progress to the referrer?

Yes. Providing clinical updates and reporting progress against agreed milestones to the referrer is a core part of the Rehab-in-the-Home protocol.
