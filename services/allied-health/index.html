Common Questions

Allied Health FAQs

What happens in an OT assessment?

An Occupational Therapist looks at your physical function and your home environment together. They identify specific barriers and clinical opportunities to help you live more safely and independently, providing a clear roadmap of practical recommendations.

Can OT work with my existing supports?

Absolutely. With your consent, we coordinate our clinical recommendations with your Community Care team, family members, and medical referrers. This ensures everyone is working toward the same set of functional goals.

Do you offer other allied health disciplines?

Currently, our Allied Health services include Occupational Therapy, Physiotherapy and Exercise Physiology. We focus on these core disciplines as they are critical for the hospital-to-home transition and maintaining functional independence at home.
