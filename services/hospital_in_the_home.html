Clinical Clarifications

HIH FAQs

Is HIH the same as community nursing?

No. Hospital-in-the-Home is protocol-led hospital-substitute care for defined pathways and cohorts. It involves higher intensity clinical governance and coordination than general community nursing.

What happens if a client deteriorates?

We follow strictly defined escalation pathways agreed upon with the referrer. This may include immediate transfer back to hospital or coordination with urgent services depending on the situation.

Is HIH available in every region?

Availability varies by region and clinical readiness. We recommend contacting our team or submitting a referral to confirm availability in your specific area.
