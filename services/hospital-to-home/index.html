Common Questions

Hospital to Home FAQs

How soon can you start support?

We aim to be highly responsive to hospital discharge dates. Early referral (ideally 48-72 hours before discharge) allows our team to better prepare the home environment and coordinate clinical staffing.

Is this a long-term service?

Hospital to Home is designed as bridging support. Our goal is to stabilise your recovery and functional independence. If you require long-term support, we provide a structured handover into our Community Care or NDIS-funded divisions.

Can I request this as a family member?

Yes. You can start the process by clicking Get Support. We will then coordinate with the hospital team to confirm the clinical requirements and ensure a safe pathway is established.
