Respect & Privacy

Your Rights in
True Care

We welcome all feedback and take complaints seriously. Our goal is to respond respectfully, clearly, and fairly to every interaction.

Give Feedback Make a Complaint
Your Rights Feedback Complaints External Support

Your basic rights

  • 1 To be treated with absolute dignity and clinical respect.
  • 2 To full privacy and secure information handling.
  • 3 To ask questions and understand your care pathway.
  • 4 To provide feedback without fear of disadvantage.
Our Commitment

What happens next

1

We Acknowledge

We aim to confirm receipt of your feedback or complaint immediately and outline the expected investigation timeline.

2

We Review & Act

Our quality team assesses what happened, identifies required improvements, and documents necessary changes to our clinical processes.

3

We Respond

Where appropriate, we provide a formal response detailing the outcomes of our review and the actions taken to resolve your concern.

This form is NOT for emergencies. If you are in immediate danger, please dial 000.

Make a Complaint
