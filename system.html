Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and handle personal information with the highest level of clinical integrity. This document outlines our data standards for the Cross Care clinical ecosystem.

Data Collection

We collect information provided during intake and clinical transition tasks, including clinical records and assessment notes required for safe discharge.

Data Security

Cross Care Group is a Microsoft-first organization. All clinical data is stored in healthcare-standard encrypted environments with multi-factor audit paths.

Clinical Disclosure

Information may be shared only with defined care team members (Doctors, OT, Registered Nurses) as part of your integrated care plan. We never disclose data for marketing purposes.

Your Rights

You have the right to request a copy of your clinical data held by the Cross Care Group at any time. Requests are processed within 48 business hours.

Note: This is a design prototype of our policy framework. For formal legal documents, please contact our clinical governance team at admin@crosscaregroup.com.au.

Terms of Use

These terms govern the use of the Cross Care Group digital ecosystem and our clinical communication framework.

1. Clinical Information

Information on this site provides a general roadmap for care. It is not a substitute for active clinical assessment by your specific Cross Care Registered Nurse or Allied Health provider.

2. Professional Referral

Our referrer portals are intended for healthcare professionals. Submissions made via these paths are subject to professional verification protocols.

3. Liability

While we strive for absolute accuracy, Cross Care Group is not liable for technical delays in data transmission beyond our enterprise security environment.

Cross Care Group reserves the right to update these terms as clinical mandates evolve.
