Easy Read

Simple information for you

Easy Read uses simple words and short sentences. This helps you understand what we do and how we can help.

Currently providing care across QLD, NSW, SA, WA and TAS.

What we do How to get help

What we do

We help people live safely and happily at home.

Support at home

We help with daily living and nursing care if you need it.

Therapy

We provide therapy, like Occupational Therapy, to help you do more.

Leaving hospital

We help you move safely from hospital back to your home.

How to Get Help

We are here to help you every step of the way.

How to start

  • 1

    Tell us what you need. You can call us or use our website.

  • 2

    We will talk with you about your goals.

  • 3

    We make a plan together to help you at home.

Get Support Now Call 1300 591 861

What happens next

We will visit you to see how you are doing. We want to make sure you are safe and happy.

You can always have a family member or friend with you when we talk.
