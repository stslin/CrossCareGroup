Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services, funding, and how we support you.

Currently providing care across QLD, NSW, SA, TAS and WA.

Get Support Contact Us

01 Getting Started

What is the simplest way to start?
Start on our Get support page. Tell us what’s happening and what you need next. We’ll follow up and explain the next step in plain language.

What happens after I submit a form?
We review what you’ve shared, confirm suitability and availability, and contact you if we need more detail. If we’re not the right fit, we’ll tell you early and discuss options.

What information should I include?
Share the essentials and your goals. Include your location and preferred contact details. If you’re unsure what to include, submit what you can and we’ll ask any follow-up questions.

Where do you currently operate?

We currently support clients across QLD, NSW, SA, WA and TAS. Availability may vary—contact us if you’re unsure.

02 Services

What services do you offer?

We provide Community Care and Allied Health, and we are building structured hospital-to-home programs. Explore all services on our Services page.

What is Community Care?
Community Care covers practical in-home supports, and can include skilled nursing where clinically appropriate. It supports safe, consistent living at home.

View Community Care

What is Allied Health?
Allied Health supports function and independence through assessment and therapy. (Currently Occupational Therapy.)

View Allied Health

What are high-intensity supports?
High-intensity supports are more complex supports that require additional competence, supervision and safety planning. Availability depends on scope, capability and location. If you’re not sure, start with Get support.

03 Funding

Do you support NDIS participants?
Yes. We support NDIS participants and can confirm service fit during intake.

Do you accept self-funded clients?
We can discuss options and confirm what services are suitable. Use Get support to start.

What if I’m not sure what funding type I have?
That’s okay. Choose “Not sure” on the Get support form and we’ll clarify it with you.

04 Hospitals & Referrers

How do I make a referral?
Use the online referral form on our Referral page.

What information helps triage a referral?
The essentials include: reason for referral, risks, equipment needs (if any), location, and preferred contact details. Share only what’s necessary and ensure consent/authority to share information.

Can you support discharge pathways?
We are designed to support hospital-to-home transitions by coordinating community support and allied health inputs around one plan. Availability depends on location and service fit.

Go to For hospitals & referrers

05 Privacy

How do you protect privacy?

Privacy and consent are non-negotiable. We collect and use information to deliver services and respond to enquiries, and we aim to share only what is necessary.

See our Privacy policy for more detail.

Should I upload sensitive documents in a web form?
Only share the minimum necessary information. If additional documents are required, we’ll confirm the safest pathway during intake or referral.

06 Feedback & Complaints

How do I give feedback?
You can share feedback on our Rights, feedback & complaints page. You can provide feedback anonymously if you prefer.

How do I make a complaint?
You can make a complaint on the Rights, feedback & complaints page. We’ll explain what happens next and respond respectfully.

Will giving feedback affect my support?
No. You can raise concerns without being treated unfairly. Feedback helps us improve.

Still have questions?

Our team is here to help you navigate your care options.
